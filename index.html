About IoT Transforms

IoT Transforms Ltd started in 2016. Its founders worked with major companies as Industry Solutions consultants for IoT, Industry 4.0 and the Industrial Internet. They saw the possibilities for IoT in a much more impactful way than large IT companies selling products and services wrapped around those products; for them IoT was a means to revenue rather than pushing society and nudging capitalism to its next stage of evolution. The founders of IoT Transforms are focused on surfing the next waves of an Industrial Revolution and helping companies and organisations to take advantage of new IT innovation in an “inclusive way”. This is about involving people in the Board Room and at the coal face in the act of creating new value. IoT Transforms Ltd is truly committed to Value Creation that benefits share holders, employees, customers and society at large. IoT Transforms sees “quick win” exploitation of one stakeholder group by another as the root of many problems in the modern world. We want to unlock IoT creativity WITH people, rather than doing it TOO them in ways that naturally triggers resistance and disfunctionality. As such, we want to work with enlightened customers that share a desire to constructively and intelligently evolve our organisations.

Abstract Data Model

Just because you understand friction causes heat, don’t assume IT experts have the same domain knowledge.

In our “IoT Abstract Data Model” Package we will:

  • Agree a Statement of Work and submit a price.
  • Understand the relationship between meta-data and work flow activities in the physical world.

IoT Use Cases

To get comparable quotes from the IT industry you need a common definition of what would be needed so different vendors quote on the same basis. This allows comparison between competing suppliers. However, you understand how to create business value better than IT experts, so you need to help them.

In our “IoT Use Cases” Package we will:

  • Agree a Statement of Work and submit a price.
  • Map a work flow and the functionality embedded in it.
  • Innovate the work flow with IoT possibilities.
  • Agree IT performance boundaries.
  • Craft one or more Use Cases we can use as part of the procurement of IT.
  • Explain the Use Case to IT suppliers quoting for the work so everyone is on the same page from the start.

Outcome: Develop a way to allow competing suppliers to bring their best solutions in ways that allow one vendor to be compared with another.
