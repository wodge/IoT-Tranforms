About IoT Transforms

IoT Transforms Ltd started in 2016. Its founders cut their teeth in IoT, Industry 4.0 and the Industrial Internet. However, they saw the possibilities for IoT in a much more impactful way than large IT companies which stuck to selling products and services wrapped around those products; for them IoT was a means to revenue rather than pushing society and capitalism to its next stage of evolution. The founders of IoT Transforms are focused on pushing the next stage of an Industrial Revolution and helping companies and organisations to take advantage of new IT innovation in an “inclusive way”. This is about involving people at the coal face in the act of creating new value (i.e. value that did not exist previously). IoT Transforms Ltd is truly committed to Value Creation that benefits share holders, employees, customers and society at large. IoT Transforms sees “quick win” exploitation of one stakeholder group by another as the root of may problems in the modern world and seeks to work with enlightened customers that share this desire to evolve our organisations
